Sumra Latif Mughal Treasury Front Office Lead

Sumra Latif Mughal is a seasoned treasury and banking professional with over 12 years of experience across treasury management, liquidity optimization, corporate finance, financial markets, and digital banking. Throughout her career, she has contributed to the growth and transformation of leading financial institutions by delivering strategic treasury solutions, strengthening financial governance, and driving operational excellence.

Currently serving as Treasury Front Office Lead at HugoBank, she is responsible for establishing and leading treasury front-office functions for one of Pakistan's emerging digital banking platforms — spanning liquidity management, balance sheet optimization, funding strategy, money markets, investment management, and treasury risk oversight.

Her professional journey spans HugoBank, Telenor Microfinance Bank, ICBC Pakistan, and Sindh Bank, where she built deep expertise in treasury operations, foreign exchange, money markets, regulatory compliance, and corporate finance.
